About Study Buddy SRT

Your personal tool for smarter learning with spaced repetition

Why Use Study Buddy SRT?

Study Buddy SRT is designed to help you retain knowledge more effectively using the principle of spaced repetition. This method ensures that you review topics at optimal intervals, making learning more efficient and long-lasting.

Whether you are a student preparing for exams, a professional learning new skills, or just someone who wants to remember more of what they study, Study Buddy SRT provides a simple and interactive way to track your progress and schedule revisions.

Key Benefits

  • Track Your Progress: Keep a clear record of topics and revision dates for each user.
  • Automatic Revision Schedule: The app calculates optimal review intervals for you.
  • Easy to Use: Add topics quickly and see your upcoming revisions at a glance.
  • Personalized Experience: Save your selected user and agenda automatically with localStorage.
  • Clean and Intuitive Design: Focus on your studies with minimal distractions.

Get Started

Simply go back to the Home Page, select a user, and start adding topics to your agenda. Study Buddy SRT will take care of the rest, helping you study smarter, not harder!
